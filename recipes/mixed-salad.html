Mixed Salad

>An healty alternative to heavy meals

Most of us suffer of overcaloric nutrition, which is no suprise since our meals are often highly processed and full of fat and sugar. An handmade salad is a sane alternative to that and everybody can prepare it.

Salads should contain plant-based, unprocessed, fresh products. Even if this recipe contains certain things, your salad can be made of very different vegetable, fruit and seeds. Do not hesitate to be creative and design your very own health-booster.

Ingredients

  • Letuce
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Orange pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash lettuce, cucumber, tomato and orange pepper
  2. Cut the cucumber into small pieces
  3. Quarter the tomato. Then quarter these pieces again
  4. Pick the lettuce to pieces
  5. Mix salt, pepper, oil and vinegar
  6. Mix the vegetables and pour the sauce over it
