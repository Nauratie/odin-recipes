How to make Curd you can eat at any time of the day

This delicious meal provides carbohydrates, healthy fats, protein, minerals and vitamins of all kinds. Actually it contains nearly all nutritions you need.

Nurishing and easy to diggest it can be served for breakfast, lunch or dinner. In the morning it provides the necessary energy to start the day. If you need a quick meal for lunch, just prepare it the day before, store it in the fridge and you will have a nice dish that supplies you with enough power to meet the challenges of the afternoon. Furthermore, it can serve as dinner, where you load nutrients to recuperate in the night.
